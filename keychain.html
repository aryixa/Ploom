Materials:

  • Yarn (your choice of color)
  • Crochet hook (appropriate size for your yarn)
  • Scissors
  • Keychain ring
  • Yarn needle
Steps:
Step Description
1 Make a slip knot and chain 4 stitches.
2 Join the chain into a loop with a slip stitch.
3 Chain 3 (counts as the first double crochet), then work 11 double crochets into the loop.
4 Join with a slip stitch into the top of the initial chain-3.
5 Repeat step 3 to create additional petals around the circle.
6 Cut yarn and weave in ends.
7 Attach the keychain ring to your flower using a yarn needle.
